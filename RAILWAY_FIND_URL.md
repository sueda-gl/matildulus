## Your site is deployed! Now get the public URL:

### Option 1: Networking Tab (Easiest)
1. On the Railway dashboard, look at the **top tabs** next to "Architecture"
2. Click **"Settings"** tab (or "Networking" if you see it)
3. Scroll to **"Networking"** or **"Domains"** section
4. You'll see:
   - **"Public Domain"** - Click "Generate Domain" if you don't see one yet
   - Or you'll see a URL like: `matildulus-production.up.railway.app`
   - **Copy that URL** - that's your live site!

### Option 2: Service Overview
1. Click on your **"matildulus"** service (on the left sidebar or main panel)
2. Look for a **"Domains"** or **"Networking"** section in the details
3. Find the public domain URL there

### Option 3: Settings Tab
1. Click **"Settings"** tab at the top
2. Look for **"Networking"** or **"Domains"** section
3. You should see your public domain or a button to generate one

### Option 4: Quick Access
1. In the Railway dashboard, sometimes the URL appears in:
   - The service card on the left sidebar
   - The top header of the service details
   - A green/blue link button

---

## What the URL looks like:
- Format: `matildulus-production.up.railway.app`
- Or: `sparkling-hope-production.up.railway.app` (based on your project name)
